About us Contact Get Involved What We Do

Helping impoverished youth, and schools, explore the wonders of experimental science and engineering.

We are committed to one ideal, and one ideal alone: Every person has a right to science, just as they have a right to life and liberty.

Why are we here?

The founders of this organization come from a relatively impoverished
city and attended a high school that had a weak science program. When
attending college for a degree in engineering, they realized just how
far behind they were- in both academics and research experience- in
comparison to those that grew in a more affluent environment. The stress
of playing "catch-up" was unrelenting and monumental.

We are an organization designed to lessen, if not eradicate, this disparity
of scientific experience and knowledge. Join us and join the crusade to expose
young minds to the amazing world of experimental science and engineering.
